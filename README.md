# UO Forum Archive

A searchable archive of the official UO.com forums (Ultima Online), built with SvelteKit and Pagefind.

**Live site**: https://gh.fspy.net/uo-forum-archive/

## What happened to the forums?

On February 23, 2026, Broadsword announced the sunset of the UO.com forums, effective February 27, 2026. Community discussions were moved to the [Official Ultima Online Discord](https://discord.com/invite/ultimaonline). This was at least the third or fourth time the forums have been shut down over the game's nearly three-decade history.
